About Me:

My name is Matt Keplinger and I live in Savannah, Georgia. I was in the United States Army for 10 years as a bomb technician and served multiple combat tours.

I earned a B.S. in Information Technology from Georgia Southern University in July of 2021. Currently, I am a full-stack development student at devCodeCamp to obtain the training and knowledge required to be a Software Developer. Prior to devCodeCamp, I worked as a Systems Analyst and Junior Developer for Nine Line Apparel while finishing my degree.

I enjoy anything that stimulates my mind. My main hobbies include auto racing, traveling with my family, and reverse engineering anything I can find (cars, computers, etc.).
