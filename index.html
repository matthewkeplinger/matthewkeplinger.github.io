About Me:

My name is Matt Keplinger and I live in Savannah, Georgia. I was in the United States Army for 10 years as a bomb technician and served multiple combat tours. I have a B.S. in Information Technology from Georgia Southern University. I am currently a full-stack development student at devCodeCamp, learning Javascript, Python, SQL, HTML/CSS, and other languages. I worked as a Systems Analyst and Junior Developer for Nine Line Apparel while finishing my degree. I enjoy programming challenges and anything that stimulates my mind. My other hobbies include auto racing, my family, and video games.
